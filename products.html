About Us

About Workshop.co At Workshop.co, we believe that fresh, healthy food should be easy to access, simple to enjoy, and never a compromise. We’re a modern fruit and grocery delivery service on a mission to bring the goodness of the market straight to your home—without the hassle. Our journey began with a single goal: to make everyday food shopping feel less like a chore and more like a joy. We partner with local farmers, trusted growers, and ethical suppliers to bring you the very best of the season—handpicked fruits, crisp vegetables, and a thoughtfully curated range of pantry staples, snacks, dairy, and more. Everything we offer is chosen with care, so you can shop with confidence knowing it’s fresh, clean, and delicious. At the heart of Workshop.co is a deep respect for quality, community, and sustainability. We believe in supporting local producers and minimizing waste, using eco-friendly packaging whenever possible and reducing our environmental footprint every step of the way. Our online platform is designed to make grocery shopping easier than ever—with a clean, intuitive experience, customizable orders, and flexible delivery options that fit your schedule. Whether you're cooking for your family, prepping for the week, or just need a few essentials, Workshop.co is here to help you save time and eat better. From farm to fridge, we handle every step with care—so you can enjoy the simple pleasure of truly fresh food.
